Book Bag 

Sometimes,

my friends and I talk about books.

We bargain and barter, exchanging one for another--

shopping from each other,

bookshelves, stacks, and crates.

Acquired from holidays, the book loft, and each other. 

Sometimes covered in dust bunnies

and hair that seems to be everywhere. 

That's what happens when you live with eight people.

 

We loan each other

easy reads

that fly by on a friday night.

We loan each other

meaningfulness

and thoughts.

I love that we do both.
